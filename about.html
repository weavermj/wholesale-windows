About Wholesale Windows

At Wholesale Windows we are a family-run company, with managing director John Gerrard, wife Jean and daughter Donna all part of the daily working of our business. As such, we are extremely proud of our 15 years serving the people of Wirral and the North West, and we look to continue this into the future.

Since inception, here at Wholesale Windows we have been supplying both domestic and trade customers, providing exceptional quality products, skilled and professional workmanship, technical expertise and highly-skilled staff.

We have transformed the look of many homes and businesses across the North West.

Our product range of ‘A-rated’ windows is extensive, with all units available in a wide array of styles and colours, including traditional white, oak, rosewood and various other finishes.

Conservatories, orangeries and various coloured bi-fold and garage doors are also available. So, whatever your individual requirements, you’re sure to find what you’re looking for.

All of our products are BSI Kite Marked and carry a full, 10-year insurance-backed guarantee. Coupled with our friendly and experienced fitting teams, you can rest assured you’re in safe hands. And, as a CERTASS-registered company, you can be certain Wholesale Windows adheres to all building regulations.

By supplying and installing PVC-u window and door systems, we can guarantee you:

Long service life - materials made to last;

Maintenance free - no regular re-painting;

Choice - wide variety of designs & styles;

Security - high strength window & door profiles;

Insulation - thermal and sound;

Quality fitting - by our own team of experts;

After sales - ten year insurance backed guarantee.

When you contact Wholesale Windows you will realise just how easy and affordable it is to achieve your ideal home. As home improvement specialists, we believe in putting you, our customers, first and giving you a product that will give you many years of happiness and pleasure.
